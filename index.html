Smart meal planning for complex dietary needs.

What is Recette?

Following a diet for a health condition (like heart disease, diabetes, or food allergies) is difficult. Often, you're juggling multiple, conflicting rules at once.

Recette is an AI-powered mobile app designed to do the hard work for you. It helps you find, analyze, and plan meals that actually fit your unique and complex nutritional requirements, giving you confidence and control over your health.

Planned Features

  • AI-Powered Recipe Analysis: Instantly analyze any recipe for compliance with multiple dietary profiles (e.g., low-sodium, low-fat, and diabetic-friendly at the same time).
  • Smart Recipe Search: Find recipes from across the web that are pre-filtered to match your specific health needs.
  • Meal Planning & Grocery Lists: Easily build weekly meal plans and generate corresponding grocery lists.
  • Recipe Scanning (OCR): Take a photo of a recipe from a book or magazine, and Recette will digitize and analyze it for you.
